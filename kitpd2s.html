About KITPD2s

Pioneering research and innovation at the intersection of mechanical engineering and modern technology

Who We Are

KITPD2s is a distinguished research society under the KIIT School of Mechanical Engineering, renowned for its groundbreaking work in various technological domains. Our society serves as a hub for innovation, bringing together brilliant minds to solve real-world problems.

With our prestigious collaboration with the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), Govt. of India, we've established ourselves as a leader in research and development, producing numerous patents and research papers that contribute to technological advancement.
